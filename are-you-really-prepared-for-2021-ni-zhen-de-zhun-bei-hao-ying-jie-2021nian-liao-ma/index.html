Are you really prepared for 2021? 你真的準備好迎接2021年了嗎?

October 18, 2021 by Wending Mai

2020對我們每個人來説都是不平常的一年.

在這辭舊迎新的時候,許多人都想告別悲傷的2020,把希望寄托在未來. 他們仿佛以爲2020年只是一場意外,是歷史上升過程中一個”V”字型間斷點. 可事實上,隨著全球化時代的紅利漸行漸遠,未來的世界也許再也不會再次回到那個空前繁榮30年. 2020年已經過去,但是,你真的準備好迎接2021年,去體驗那即將來臨的暴風雨了嗎?

在此,引用Franklin D. Roosevelt 總統的一句話與大家共勉: A smooth sea never made a skilled sailor.

2020 is an extraordinary year for each of us. At this time of new year’s eve, many people want to say goodbye to the sad past and place their hopes in the future. They seem to think that the year 2020 is just an accident, a “V”-shaped discontinuity in the upward process of history. But in fact, as the dividends of the globalization era drift away, the future world may never return to that unprecedented prosperity for past 30 years. 2020 has passed, but are you really ready to welcome 2021 and prepared to experience the coming storm?

Here, I want to share one of my favourate quote from President Franklin D.Roosevelt:

A smooth sea never made a skilled sailor.
