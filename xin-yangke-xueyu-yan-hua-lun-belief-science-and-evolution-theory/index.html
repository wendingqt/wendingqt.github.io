    信仰,科學,與演化論 Belief, science, and evolution theory

    最近看了阿姨最新的視頻,很有感觸.

    最核心的科學其實就是純信仰,而且是一種典型的一神教信仰.它是由以下兩個基本觀念構成的:第一,世界具有秩序;第二,秩序具有普世性.這兩個基本前提像歐幾里得的四個公設一樣,不証自明.如果不承認這兩個公設的話,科學也就全都沒有意義. 許多落後部落的迷信宗教并不承認世界秩序存在.他們的世界是由各種亂七八糟,自相矛盾的精靈或者偶像構成的.這種世界觀缺乏内在的邏輯性,是完全破碎性的. 另外,爲什麽規律一定要是普遍性的?注意,演化論和科學的差異就在這裏:演化論沒有這個”秩序具有普遍性”這個信仰在裏面.演化論容許世界存在許多局部的秩序,但是并沒有一統的秩序.這樣的局部性秩序是物理學家一般不會接受的.局部規律和普遍規律是有差別的. 什麽是科學?科學是一種普遍系統的建構能力.當你看到這個複雜而混亂的世界,仿佛沒有秩序,但隱隱約約背後有存在某種神秘秩序.你有一種愛因斯坦式的願望:混亂是不美的,像哥白尼體系一樣簡潔明瞭的東西才是真理.你想要從紛繁萬象的複雜現象中抽離出這個簡單的真理.這樣的願望一般被稱之爲宗教,但真是這種東西才是科學的本體,而其它技術性的東西其實都是枝枝葉葉性質的. 技術層面的東西,演化史生態性的,方向不可預測.演化無方向,演化沒有優劣性,演化優勢只是適應而已.而適應(存在)本身是一個偶然性的東西.因此,演化理論能夠解釋一切,但是它不具備製造普適理論的.演化論是一種路徑描繪,而不是解釋.就像歷史研究一樣,描繪曾經如何如何,但是永遠無法達到牛頓愛因斯坦曾經追索過的”發現世界萬事萬物的内在關聯”這樣一種基本層次. 演化論不能夠給人提供安慰和解釋的那種宗教性的渴求,也不能夠給科學家提供那種從背後找出規律的快樂.那種像是迷了心竅,早聞道夕死可,認識了規律本身就像宗教徒認識到上帝,跟上帝或者宇宙精神結合一樣.這種至高無上的大快樂是人世間一切小快樂都不能夠比擬的.

    The core of science is actually pure belief, and it is a typical monotheistic belief. It is composed of the following two basic concepts: first, the world has order; second, the order is universal. These two basic axioms are like Euclid’s four axioms as self-evident. If these two axioms are not acknowledged, it is meaningless to discuss about science. Many superstitious religions of backward tribes do not recognize the existence of a world order. Their world is made up of all kinds of messy, self-contradictory elves or idols. This kind of world view lacks inherent logic and is completely broken. Someone might ask, why must the order be universal? Note that the difference between the evolution theory and science is here: the evolution theory does not have the belief that “order has universality” in it. The theory of evolution allows many partial orders in the various domains of the world, but it does not accept an unified order. Such a local order is generally not accepted by physicists. What is science? Science is the ability to construct a universal system. When you see this complex and chaotic world, there seems to be no order, but there is a certain mysterious order behind it. If you have an Einsteinian desire: Chaos is not beautiful. Truth is what is as simple and clear as the Copernican system. You want to extract this simple truth from the complex phenomenon. Such a desire is generally called religion, but that is true core of science. On the other hand, technical and engineering stuffs are actually branches from the core. At the technical level, evolution history is ecological. Its direction is unpredictable. The evolutionary advantages are merely adaptations. However, adaptation (existence) itself is an accidental thing. Therefore, evolution theory can explain everything. But it does not have the ability to create a system conveys the universal order. The evolution theory is a path naration instead of an explanation. Just like historical study, it describes what happened in the past, but it can never achieve the abition that Newton Einstein once pursued:”Discovering the inner connection of everything in the world”. The theory of evolution cannot provide people with the kind of religious desire for comfort and explanation, nor can it provide scientists with the kind of happiness of discovering the laws behind. That kind of happyness is like a religious believer who recognizes God and integrates with God or the spirit of the universe. This kind of supreme great happiness is far beyond all the other small happiness in the world.
